About the club

We welcome beginners and experienced builders. Through peer-led sessions and mentor support, members learn AI foundations, prompt engineering, model deployment, and ethical AI practices.

What makes us different

  • Project-first learning from week one
  • Cross-disciplinary teams from all faculties
  • Focus on responsible AI and local relevance
